EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

THIS REPORT DETAILS THE CONSTRUCTION OF A BENCH CARRIED OUT BY COHORT 7 OF CHANGE FOOD FOR GOOD'S INTRO TO AGTECH PROGRAM AT EL COQUI LIBERATION COMMUNITY GARDEN IN THE BRONX, NY. THIS BUILD CONSTITUTED PART ONE OF A THREE-DAY CONSTRUCTION CRASH COURSE.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

THE GOAL OF THIS PROJECT IS FOR MEMBERS OF THE COHORT TO PRACTICE BASIC CONSTRUCTION SKILLS USING HAND SAWS AND DRILLS, AS WELL AS PROVIDE THE COMMUNITY GARDEN WITH ADDITIONAL SEATING FOR EVENTS AND COMMUNITY MEMBERS.

PROJECT ANALYSIS

BECAUSE THE BENCH WAS TO BE PLACED IN AN OUTDOOR SETTING, WE DESIGNED A QUITE HEAVY AND DURABLE BENCH TO WITHSTAND THE CONDITIONS. IT WAS ALSO A QUITE SIMPLISTIC DESIGN, OPTING FOR FUNCTIONALITY AND EASE OF CONSTRUCTION OVER AESTHETICS.

CHALLENGES

THE COHORT RAN INTO CHALLENGES PUTTING ALL THE PIECES TOGETHER FOR THE FINAL BENCH BECAUSE THERE WAS SOME MISCOMMUNICATION AND LACK OF CLARITY AROUND HOW INDIVIDUAL PIECES SHOULD BE ASSEMBLED. THIS MEANT READJUSTING THE FINAL COMING TOGETHER OF THE PIECES TO CREATE THE BENCH.

SOLUTIONS

BY TAKING A STEP BACK AND ASSESSING THE INDIVIDUAL COMPONENTS THAT WE HAD BUILT AND ADJUSTING OUR CONSTRUCTION PLAN, WE WERE ABLE TO PUT TOGETHER A FUNCTIONAL FINAL PRODUCT.

CONCLUSION

WHILE THE RESULTING BENCH DID LOOK HOW IT WAS INTENDED TO LOOK, THERE WERE SOME COMPLICATIONS THAT COULD HAVE BEEN SMOOTHED OUT WITH CLEARER COMMUNICATION DURING THE BUILD. ULTIMATELY, THE DESIGN FOR THE BENCH IS QUITE EFFECTIVE BECAUSE IT IS VERY STURDY AND DURABLE, WHICH IS IMPORTANT AS IT WILL BE PLACED IN AN OUTDOOR SETTING AND WILL ENDURE VARYING WEATHER CONDITIONS. THE BUILD WAS ALSO SUCCESSFUL IN THE SENSE THAT ALL MEMBERS OF THE COHORT WERE ABLE TO PRACTICE USING A HAND SAW TO CUT WOOD PLANKS TO SIZE, AS WELL AS PRACTICE USING A DRILL TO ATTACH CUT WOOD PIECES TOGETHER AND EVENTUALLY BRING THE WHOLE BENCH TOGETHER. FOR FUTURE BUILDS, I WOULD OPT FOR A MORE AESTHETIC DESIGN THAT COULD UTILIZE THE SKILLS PRACTICED ACROSS ALL THREE CONSTRUCTION DAYS.
